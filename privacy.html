Clipmaxxer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Clipmaxxer ("we", "the app") helps streamers turn their livestream highlights into short-form videos and publish them to connected social platforms. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • TikTok account data — when you connect your TikTok account via TikTok Login, we receive a basic account identifier (open_id) and an access token scoped to uploading content. We use TikTok's user.info.basic and video.upload scopes only.
  • Video content — the clips you choose to generate and publish, plus their captions and titles.
  • Source data — public stream/VOD identifiers and publicly available clip metadata (view counts, timestamps) used to find highlight moments.

How we use it

  • To upload the clips you create to your own TikTok Drafts inbox or, where you explicitly opt in, to publish them to your TikTok account.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the clip-generation features you use.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • We do not post to your account without your action or explicit opt-in.
  • We do not use your TikTok access token for anything other than uploading the clips you generate.

Data sharing

We share content with TikTok only to fulfill your upload/publish request, via TikTok's official Content Posting API. We do not share your data with other third parties except as required to operate the service (e.g. cloud hosting) or by law.

Data retention & deletion

You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time, which revokes our access token. You may request deletion of any data we hold about you by contacting us at the email below; we will delete it within 30 days.
